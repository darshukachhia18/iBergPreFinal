About Us

iBerg Designs

iBerg Designs is a start-up founded in 2020 by Rahil Lathigra. Our company is known for creating digitally creative products for our clients. We strive to provide all kinds of services related to design and anything that will help your business accelerate. iBerg largely focuses on user experience and visual designs. We ensure timely delivery of all our digital products, making sure they are of the utmost quality.

Why would you invest in us ?

The motto of iBerg Designs is to transform lives through technology. iBerg as a company is extremely versatile as we work with clients on every budget and aim to be accessible to our clients. Your projects are highly safe with us as we maintain the confidentiality of contracts. We take pride in saying that we maintain strong and transparent communication with our clients.
