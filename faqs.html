FAQs

What is the reason behind naming your company “iBerg Designs”?

iBerg is derived from the word iceberg, the concept that we just see the icebergs surface, but whats beneath we aren’t aware until exposed to, the company’s moto is to let its users and clients be aware of the entire process behind building a product, and not just see what it is delivered to them, that’s why iBerg Designs!

What makes your company different?

We don’t sell, we serve people and we help people solve their problems through digital products. However that isn’t all that makes us different, we only serve people our products when we ourselves have used it or bought it. Afterall we don’t want our clients using our services and products that we ourself wouldn’t buy.

What domains do you have expertise in?

iBerg focuses on the entire market of design, our diversified services pretty much makes us expertise in every field of business like, education, tech, finance, food etc.

How much do you charge for your services?

Our service charges depends on each product designed, however we do consider our clients budget and then provide the final negotiated charge.

What if I need help? Do you offer support?

Yes, we believe in staying accessible to our clients, so we will always offer our help and support whenever the client approaches us on business hours.

What clients have you worked for?

iBerg works with clients of every budget, and every field of interest, as we believe in growing businesses ranging from the smallest scale to the biggest.

Can you help me with online marketing?

iYes, our team of digital marketers will provide all the help to our clients.

Will you write the text for my website?

Yes, our specialized content creators will be in collaboration with you during the whole process of time.

Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes, we redesign the entire product according to the latest trends and technologies.

Are your websites custom? Do you use templates?

Our every product is custom. iBerg believes in providing organic work, our team of designers sit and design the entire product from scratch, as we want our client to have their product of a personalised taste.

Will you provide me with a prototype of my presentations?

Yes, we always prefer to test our product before actually sending it to the development process, each product designed will be prototyped and tested with our user testing team and clients.

How long do you take to deliver the finished product?

Our deliverables totally depend on its project length and client’s deadline, however we ensure our client’s to deliver the products on time and with utmost quality.
